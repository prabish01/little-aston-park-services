Our Services
We offer a wide range of garden and outdoor maintenance services designed to keep your space looking its best right across the year. From routine care like mowing and hedge trimming to more involved projects like garden clearances, planting and landscaping, our expert team is here to provide you with a tailored and high-quality service.

Take a look at some of the services we specialise in below and understand how we can transform, refine and maintain your green space, no matter how big or small.

Garden Maintenance
Lawn Care: We provide comprehensive lawn care services, including mowing, edging, scarification, aeration, overseeding, fertilising, and weed treatment to keep your lawn healthy, green, and well-maintained.
Hedge Trimming and Shaping: Our hedge cutting services ensure your hedges remain neat, well-shaped, and healthy, enhancing both appearance and structure.
Weeding and Border Maintenance: We remove weeds from beds, borders, and pathways, applying mulch where needed to help suppress regrowth and retain moisture.
Pruning and Shaping: We carefully prune shrubs, roses, perennials, and small trees to encourage healthy growth, improved structure, and seasonal flowering.
General Tidying and Cleaning: From leaf clearance to sweeping and debris removal, we keep your garden looking clean, organised, and well-presented.
Seasonal Maintenance: We offer tailored seasonal services, including spring preparation, summer upkeep, autumn leaf clearance, and winter tidy-ups.
One-Off and Clearance Services
Garden Clearance and Renovation: We restore overgrown or neglected gardens by removing unwanted vegetation, clearing waste, and preparing the space for future use or redesign.
Strimming and Edging: We tackle long grass, brambles, and rough areas using professional equipment to quickly bring spaces back under control.
Gutter Cleaning: We clear leaves and debris from gutters to help prevent blockages and maintain proper drainage around your property.
Planting and Design Services
Planting: We supply and install a variety of plants, including flowers, shrubs, and trees, offering guidance on suitable choices for your garden and local conditions.
Garden Design and Soft Landscaping: We assist with planning layouts, creating borders, laying turf or gravel, and installing features such as raised beds to enhance your outdoor space.
Vegetable and Fruit Growing: We help set up and maintain productive garden areas, including vegetable patches and fruit trees, with advice on planting and care.
Hard Landscaping and Installation
Patio and Path Work: We clean and restore patios, paths, and driveways using professional jet washing, improving both appearance and safety.
Fencing and Gates: We offer installation, repair, and treatment of fencing and gates to improve security, privacy, and durability.
Turfing and Levelling: We lay new lawns and level uneven ground to create a smooth, attractive finish.
Pond and Water Feature Maintenance: We provide basic cleaning, planting, and maintenance for ponds and water features to keep them looking their best.
Specialist or Additional Services
Tree Surgery and Care: We carry out light tree work such as pruning and crown lifting to maintain safety and appearance.
Disease Control: We identify plant health issues and apply appropriate treatments, including environmentally friendly options where possible.
Fertilising and Feeding: We apply the correct nutrients to lawns and plants to support healthy growth and long-term vitality.
Waste Removal and Green Waste Disposal: We responsibly collect and dispose of all garden waste, leaving your property clean and tidy.
Seasonal Extras
 We offer additional services such as hanging baskets, seasonal planting, and maintenance for residential or commercial outdoor spaces.
Other Offerings
Garden Consulting and Advice: We provide expert guidance on plant selection, garden improvements, and maintenance planning to help you get the most from your outdoor space.
Container and Small-Space Gardening: We maintain and enhance balconies, courtyards, and container gardens, ensuring even smaller spaces are attractive and well cared for.
